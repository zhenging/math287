General Solution
A solution to an nnnth-order differntial equation on an interval III is called the general solution on III if it satisfies the following contions:

  1. The solution contains nnn constant c1,c2⋯,cnc_1, c_2 \cdots, c_nc1​,c2​⋯,cn​.
  2. All solutions to the differential equtions can be obtained by passing appropriate values to the constants.
