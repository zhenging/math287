#### Wronskian
The Wronskian of $f_1, f_2$ and $f_3$
$$
\begin{aligned}
W[f_1, f_2, f_3] = \begin{vmatrix}
 f_1(x) & f_2(x) & f_3(x)\\
 f'_1(x) & f'_3(x) & f'_3(x)\\
 f''_1(x) & f''_2(x) & f''_3(x)
 \end{vmatrix}
\end{aligned}
$$
If $W \neq 0$ for some point in $I$, then _independent_.
If $W = 0$ for all point in $I$, then _inconclusive_.
