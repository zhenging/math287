### 1004 The Transform of Derivatives and Solution of Initial-Value Problem
$$
\begin{aligned}
L[f'] &= \int_0^{\infty} e^{-st} f' dt\\
&= (e^{-st} f(t))\mid_0^{\infty} +  \int_0^{\infty} s e^{-st} f(t) dt\\
&= -f(0) +  s \int_0^{\infty}  e^{-st} f(t) dt\\
&= sL[f] - f(0)\\[1.5em]
L[f''] &= s^2 L[f] - sf(0) - f'(0)
\end{aligned}
$$
