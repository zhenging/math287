7.2 Eigenspace and Nondefective Matrices

Let AAA be n×nn\times nn×n matrices.

Eigenspace
Let λ\lmλ be an eigenvalue of AAA. The set of all eigenvectors of λ\lmλ, together with the zero vector, is a subspect of \R^n. We call this subspace the eigenspace of λ\lmλ.

Algebraic Multiplicity and Geometric Multiplicity
Let λ\lmλ be an eigenvalue of AAA. The multiplicity of λ\lmλ in the characteristic polynomial is called the algebraic multiplicity of λ\lmλ, The dimension of the eigenspace corresponding to λ\lmλ is called the geometric multiplicity of λ\lmλ.

Defective/Nondefective

  • Let AAA has nnn linearly independent eigenvectors, then AAA is called nondefective. Otherwise, AAA is called defective.
  • If the algebraic multiplicity equals the geometric multiplicity for each eigenvalue of AAA, then AAA is nondefective. Otherwise,AAA is defective.
