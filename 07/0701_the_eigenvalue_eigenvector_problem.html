7.1 The Eigenvalue/Eigenvector Problem

Definition

Let AAA be m×nm\times nm×n matrix. Any values of λ\lmλ for which Av=λvAv = \lm vAv=λv has nontrival solutions vvv are called eigenvalues of AAA. The corresponding vectors vvv are called eigenvectors of AAA. (also refered as characteristic value and characteristic vectors).
